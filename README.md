A simple TUI newsreader, written in haskell

# Usage
Press '?' at any time to see the available keybinds. Upon entering the application, press 'n', to create a new mailbox, and type in your desired name, and press <enter> to confirm. Afterwards press 'f' to bring up the feed view, and 'n', to add your atom/rss feed, then <enter> to confirm, and you will see the mailbox view again, and press <enter>, on the on the one you want to use. Now you can press 'r', to fetch the entries on the feeds you are subscribing to. When it comes to finding the actual feeds, I suggest viewing the source on whatever site you want to subscribe to, and searching through the text for rss.
